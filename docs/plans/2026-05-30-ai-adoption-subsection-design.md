# AI Adoption Subsection Design

## Goal

Add a dedicated `AI Adoption` subsection to the Stats Perform Engineering Manager role that communicates leadership in team-level AI adoption, the operating philosophy behind it, and the measurable adoption outcome.

## Chosen structure

- Add a new `##### AI Adoption` subsection near the end of the Stats Perform Engineering Manager block.
- Use 2 bullets: one for philosophy, environment, guardrails, and human control; one for education, skeptic enablement, and measured adoption results.

## Approved wording

```md
##### AI Adoption
- Led AI adoption in the team by treating AI as a tool rather than a shortcut: focused first on preparing the engineering environment, workflows, and guardrails needed to avoid "garbage in, garbage out," while keeping human review and control over generated outputs.
- Drove adoption through education, practical enablement, and result-based work with skeptics, moving the team from near-zero usage to a state where ~75–80% of code is generated by agents under controlled workflows.
```
