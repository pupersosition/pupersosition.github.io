# Architecture & Design Rewrite Design

## Goal

Rewrite the `Architecture & Design` subsection in `content/cv.md` so it better communicates sustained architectural influence across team and cross-team systems, while keeping one concrete proof point.

## Chosen structure

- Keep the subsection compact with 2 bullets.
- Merge the broad architectural scope and environment coverage into the first bullet.
- Use the second bullet as a concrete, italicized `*Example of contribution:*` proof point centered on the Apache Flink decision.

## Target wording shape

1. Broad bullet describing continuous contribution to architectural design across streaming, pipeline, and service systems in both cloud and on-prem environments.
2. Concrete example showing cross-team influence, education of 3 teams, migration away from sub-optimal PoC technologies, and validation of Flink as the better choice.

## Success criteria

- Stronger emphasis on ongoing architectural leadership, not one-off system design.
- Clear cross-team scope.
- Specific and credible example.
- Concise enough to fit the existing CV style.
